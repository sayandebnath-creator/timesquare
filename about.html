About The TimeSquare Durga Puja 2025

Times Square Durga Puja is not just a religious program but a people’s celebration of Bengali & Indian culture and heritage, bringing the spirit of Durga Puja to one of the most iconic locations in the world. A festive gathering where the global Bangladeshi and Indian diaspora unite, this event bridges the community with an immersive experience that blends spirituality with the sights and sounds of Times Square. It showcases Durga Puja’s core values of unity, diversity, and devotion to Ma Durga, attracting people of all ages, nationalities, and backgrounds. Recognized by UNESCO as an Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity, Durga Pua is a celebration that transcends its religious roots to become a global symbol of community, creativity, and tradition. It is a time for devotees and enthusiasts to unite in the spirit of joy, artistic expression, and reverence for Goddess Durga, the embodiment of strength, power, and compassion.

History was Made

The Bengali Club USA organized the vibrant celebration of Durga Puja on October 5-6, 2024 in New York City, United States. As per all media houses and the people- A History was Scripted !!! A user wrote, “That's the spirit NYC, Jai Maa Durga, the respect and recognition Indians are getting abroad nowadays was never seen or given before.” Another user commented, “It is a proud moment for all Indians to see India’s culture and civilizations being respected across the world.” The Telegraph, India said “Durga Puja debuts at the heart of New York City, Times Square, with dhak and aroma of incense” Several people had even flown over from other states, like Florida and many others.
